Bicycle Colorado

Bicycle Colorado (BC) is a Denver-based nonprofit organization that has been advocating for safer and more equitable bicycling in Colorado for over 30 years. On January 1, 2023, BC acquired the nonprofit organization Colorado Cycling, formerly known as the Bicycle Racing Association of Colorado (BRAC). BRAC played a critical role in supporting amateur bicycle racing and served as the Local Association (LA) in Colorado for USA Cycling (USAC). In 2023, Bicycle Colorado serves as the LA for USAC in Colorado, and a Racing Membership with BC is included at no additional charge with the purchase of Race Membership in USAC.

Our Event Support Program assumes the role played by BRAC and supports the key stakeholders in both competitive and non-competitive cycling events. We manage a comprehensive events calendar, help events with marketing and promotion, provide advocacy support to make the sport safer, and help events manage relationships with municipalities, permitting agencies and insurance providers.
